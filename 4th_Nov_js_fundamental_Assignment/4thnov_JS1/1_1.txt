Operators in javascript:

Operators in JavaScript are symbols that perform
operations on operands. Operands can be values or variables, and operators
define how these operands interact. JavaScript supports a variety of operators,
each serving a specific purpose. 


Role of Opeators in javascript:

Operators in JavaScript play a crucial role in
performing various operations on data, making decisions, and controlling the
flow of a program. They are essential for expressing the logic and functionality
of your code in a concise and efficient manner.
